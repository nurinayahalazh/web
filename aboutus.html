Our Story

With a humble beginning of a comment section and forum, Female Daily community - with members that were considered to be early beauty adopters - grew into one of the biggest women-centered community in Indonesia. It then evolved into the first beauty review platform in Indonesia, establishing itself as top-of-mind in beauty.


In 2017 Female Daily brought the community, brands and other beauty players together in JakartaXBeauty with a staggering 15.000+ visitors, further solidifying its position at the forefront in beauty. 2020 marked another milestone as Female Daily Studio was launched,completing the cycle


The community has since grown into a powerhouse of 1.000.000+ members and 2.000.000 annual users (as of 2022), spanning all across Indonesia and abroad.


Today, Female Daily offers a 360 beauty experience: beauty review platform, editorial content, community engagement through FD Talk and events, and FD Studio commerce.
